What are the payment methods available?

We accept payment by Cash / Credit Card (Mastercard / VISA) / NETS. Full payment can be made in 3 installments over the first 3 months.

If I relocate out of the country during the first year of membership, will I be able to redeem those months back?

Yes, but you will be required to give us a one-month notice to cancel. Desire allows a maximum 3 months of redemption. Once you return, you have to show your boarding pass and passport for proof to redeem the 3 months back. Payment will be deducted as per normal according to terms and conditions agreed upon sign up.

How do I redeem my membership birthday treat?

You will receive an SMS one day before your birthday month. The birthday treat is valid for 30 days. If you are registering on your birthday month, please notify our staff or email desire-asthetics@support.com so we can manually add it into your account.

How can I cancel my membership?

We do require a minimum of 3-month commitment when you join our membership programme. After that, you can cancel with a one-month’s' notice.
